# Target Company Landscape

---

## Thesis

I fit best at the intersection of enterprise SaaS, developer-facing platforms, and AI product development. My strongest signal is taking complex, multi-stakeholder platform work — compliance, migration, activation, integrations — and turning it into measurable adoption and revenue outcomes at scale. Companies that need a Senior or Principal PM who can own a platform domain end-to-end, move fast with AI tooling, and operate credibly with both engineering and enterprise customers are the highest-conversion targets.

**Fit dimensions (3+ = Tier 1 or 2):**
1. Enterprise SaaS (5+ years directly)
2. Developer-facing platform or API products
3. AI product / AI-native features
4. Compliance / regulated environments (FedRAMP, data residency, HealthTech)
5. PLG / activation / experimentation
6. Payments / fintech platform
