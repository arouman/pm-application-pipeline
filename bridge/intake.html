Stage today's applications
Opens each built application in the staging Chrome window, attaches your PDFs, and fills in text fields. You finish dropdowns, EEO questions, and click Submit.
Job watcher
Checks your target job boards every 10 minutes and auto-builds roles that are a ≥90% fit. You'll get a macOS notification when something is ready to review.
Discover fresh companies
Mines the current Hacker News "Who is hiring?" thread for lesser-known companies (on Greenhouse / Lever / Ashby), adds the new ones to your watch list, and seeds their backlog so you're only alerted on brand-new posts. Be applicant #3, not #300.
