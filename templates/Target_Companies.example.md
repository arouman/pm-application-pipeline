## How to use tiers

Tiers are not about company prestige. They are about fit probability — how well your specific profile (skills, background, differentiators) matches what each company actually needs.

- **Tier 1** — unusually strong match on 4+ dimensions of your thesis. Apply first, with maximum tailoring effort.
- **Tier 2** — solid match on 3 dimensions. Worth tailored applications.
- **Tier 3** — partial match or uncertain fit. Apply when the role is specific and the JD signals real alignment.
- **Tier 4** — stretch or exploratory. Apply only if bandwidth allows and the role is unusually well-suited.
- **Avoid** — active reasons not to apply (culture mismatch, known layoffs, ethical conflicts, etc.).
